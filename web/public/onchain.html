SIGNA · onchain message

Write a message onto Base.

From your own wallet — OKX, Trust, MetaMask, Coinbase. No account, no SIGNA server. Open this page inside your wallet's browser, or use a desktop extension. The message lives in the transaction forever; the chain proves you sent it.

This is a single static file. Host it anywhere (GitHub Pages, IPFS, your own domain) or open it in your wallet's built-in browser — it talks straight to the chain and never sees your key. Indexer is configurable with ?indexer= and defaults to the public SIGNA node; sending costs a few cents of Base ETH for gas.
